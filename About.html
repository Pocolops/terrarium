About Us

Welcome to TropiqEden, where we specialize in creating beautiful and unique terrariums that bring nature into your home or workspace. Our team of passionate individuals share a love for nature and design.

Our team

At TropiqEden, our team is made up of passionate individuals who share a love for nature and design. We are dedicated to creating beautiful and unique terrariums that bring a little piece of nature into your life. We believe that plants have the power to improve our wellbeing and bring a sense of calm to our daily lives. Each terrarium is carefully crafted by hand using only the best materials and plants available. We offer a diverse range of terrariums to suit any style or taste, from minimalist designs to more elaborate pieces with intricate layers and textures.
