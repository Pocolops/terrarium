Terrariums

Closed Terrarium

These are sealed environments that are perfect for plants that thrive in high humidity levels. Closed terrariums require less watering and can create a self-sustaining ecosystem.

Open Terrarium

These terrariums do not have a lid and require regular watering and air circulation.

Desert Terrarium

These terrariums contain plants that are adapted to dry, arid environments, such as cacti and succulents.

Moss Terrarium

These terrariums are filled with various species of moss, which can grow in a variety of environments.
